Problem Context

Understanding the challenges in modern educational assessment

The Growing Need for Automated Assessment Systems

With the increasing reliance on digital learning resources, there is a growing need for automated systems that can generate mock tests and evaluate student responses efficiently.

Traditional mock test creation is manual, time-consuming, and often lacks adaptive feedback mechanisms that can personalize learning.

Additionally, evaluating handwritten answer scripts remains a significant challenge due to varying handwriting styles, formatting, and quality of scanned documents.

The Challenge

This limitation reduces the scalability and efficiency of existing assessment platforms, especially in educational environments where both typed and handwritten submissions are common.

Tester addresses these challenges with innovative AI solutions that streamline the entire assessment process.
